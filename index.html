About Us

Our Mission

At Sunrise Valley School, we are dedicated to providing a holistic education that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility. We believe in nurturing each student's unique potential and preparing them to be thoughtful, engaged citizens of the world.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all aspects of education.
  • Integrity: We foster honesty, respect, and ethical behavior.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and forward-thinking approaches.
  • Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity and create a welcoming environment for all.

Academic Excellence

STEM Foundations

Our STEM program equips K-12 students with critical thinking and problem-solving skills through hands-on learning in science, technology, engineering, and math.es.

  • Hands-On Experiments
  • Interactive Robotics
  • Math Games & Puzzles

Language & Literacy

Our language arts curriculum fosters a love for reading, writing, and storytelling, helping children develop strong communication skills and creativity.

  • Storytime Adventures
  • Creative Writing
  • Phonics & Vocabulary

Creative Arts

Our arts program inspires young minds to express themselves through drawing, painting, music, and drama, nurturing their imagination and confidence.

  • Art & Craft Workshops
  • Music & Movement
  • Theater & Role Play

Our Curriculum Highlights

Play-Based Learning

We believe in learning through play, where children explore, discover, and grow in a joyful and stimulating environment.

Character Building

Our curriculum emphasizes values like kindness, teamwork, and responsibility, helping children develop strong character and social skills.

Message from the Principal

Mrs. Anjali Verma

Principal

Dear Parents and Students,

Welcome to Sunrise Valley School, where we believe that education is not just about academic achievement, but about nurturing the whole child. Our mission is to create a learning environment where students develop the knowledge, skills, and character needed to thrive in an ever-changing world.

At Sunrise Valley, we are committed to excellence in education, fostering a culture of inquiry, creativity, and respect. Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to ensure that each student receives personalized attention and support to reach their full potential.

We believe in the power of partnership between home and school. Together, we can create a foundation for lifelong learning and success. I invite you to become an active part of our school community and join us in this important journey.

I look forward to meeting you and your children, and to sharing in the excitement of learning and growth that defines the Sunrise Valley experience.

Warm regards,

Anjali Verma
